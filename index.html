About Us

School's Premier Media Team

Agradhi Media Unit is the official media team of our school, dedicated to capturing and showcasing the vibrant life of our campus through various forms of media. Our talented team of students works under the guidance of experienced faculty to document school events, create promotional materials, and develop engaging content for our community.

With a passion for creativity and a commitment to excellence, we strive to provide professional-quality media services while offering students valuable hands-on experience in photography, videography, graphic design, audio production, and more.

Our Services

Our Services

Photography

Professional coverage of school events, portraits, and creative photography for various school publications and archives.

Videography

High-quality video production for school events, promotional videos, documentaries, and creative projects.

Graphic Design

Eye-catching posters, flyers, banners, social media graphics, and other visual materials for school communications.

Morning Radio

Daily broadcasts featuring school news, announcements, music, and student-created content for the entire school community.

Content Creation

Engaging content for school website, social media channels, newsletters, and other communication platforms.

Video Editing

Professional editing services for school videos, including color grading, special effects, and post-production work.

How We Work

Ideation

We brainstorm creative concepts that align with the school's vision and meet specific communication needs.

Planning

We develop detailed production plans, including schedules, equipment needs, and resource allocation.

Production

Our team executes the plan with professional equipment and techniques to capture high-quality content.

Post-Production

We carefully edit and enhance the raw content, applying creative techniques to achieve the desired outcome.

Distribution

The final content is shared through appropriate channels, including our Facebook page, school website, and physical displays.
